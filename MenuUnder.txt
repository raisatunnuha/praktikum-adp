Breakfast       : Oatmeal with pieces of fresh fruit (banana, strawberries, or blueberries), sliced almonds, and a cup of almond milk.
Morning Snack   : Low-fat Greek yogurt (150g) with added granola and pieces of fruit.
Lunch           : Vegetable salad with grilled chicken breast, mixed green leafy vegetables, tomatoes, cucumber slices with low-fat salad dressing and a slice of whole wheat bread.
Afternoon Snack : A handful of almonds or cashews.
Dinner          : A portion of grilled fish (e.g. salmon) with baked potatoes or brown rice and boiled vegetables.
Evening Snack   : A glass of almond milk or a piece of fruit.
