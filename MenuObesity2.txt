Breakfast       : Two boiled eggs or omelet with vegetables (e.g. spinach and tomatoes), a slice of toasted whole wheat bread with almond butter, and a pear.
Morning Snack   : A banana and a handful of almonds or cashews.
Lunch           : Portion of grilled chicken breast or fish with brown rice and roasted vegetables (such as carrots and broccoli), as well as a green salad with low-fat vinaigrette dressing.
Afternoon Snack : Low-fat Greek yogurt (150g) with added granola and fresh fruit pieces.
Dinner          : Grilled fish with steamed vegetables and brown rice.
Evening Snack   : A glass of almond milk or a piece of fruit.
