Breakfast       : A glass of fruit smoothie mixed with banana, strawberries, blueberries, low-fat yogurt and a little honey, and a slice of toasted whole wheat bread with almond butter.
Morning Snack   : A pear and a handful of almonds or cashews.
Lunch           : Vegetable salad with grilled chicken breast, mixed green leafy vegetables, cherry tomatoes, and cucumber slices with low-fat salad dressing and a slice of whole wheat bread.
Afternoon Snack : Low-fat Greek yogurt (150g) with added granola and fresh fruit pieces.
Dinner          : A portion of grilled fish (e.g. salmon) with baked potatoes or brown rice and boiled vegetables (such as broccoli and carrots).
Evening Snack   : A glass of almond milk or a piece of fruit.
