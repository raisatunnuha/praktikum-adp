Breakfast       : Three boiled eggs or omelet with vegetables (e.g. spinach and tomatoes), a slice of toasted whole wheat bread, and an apple.
Morning Snack   : A banana and a handful of almonds or cashews.
Lunch           : Portion of grilled chicken breast with brown rice and roasted vegetables (such as carrots and broccoli) and green salad with low-fat vinaigrette dressing.
Afternoon Snack : Low-fat Greek yogurt (150g) with added granola and fresh fruit pieces.
Dinner          : A portion of grilled fish (such as salmon) with roasted sweet potatoes and asparagus.
Evening Snack   : A glass of almond milk or a piece of fruit.
